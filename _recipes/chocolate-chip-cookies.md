---

ingredients:
    Shortening: ⅔ cup
    Butter: ⅔ cup
    Sugar: 1 cup
    Brown Sugar: 1 cup
    Eggs: 2

    Baking Soda: 1 tsp
    Salt: 1 tsp
    Chocolate Chips: 12 oz
instructions:
