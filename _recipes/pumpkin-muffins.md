---
name: Pumpkin Muffins
author: Nadene Chavet
layout: recipe
category: Breads
dry_ingredients:
    Flour: 3 cups
    Sugar: 1 cup
    Baking Powder: 4 tsp
    Salt: 1 tsp
    Cinnamon: 1 tsp
    Nutmet: 1 tsp
wet_ingredients:
    Milk: 1 cup
    Butter: ½ cup melted
    Eggs: 2
    Pumpkin: 1 cup (½ can)
instructions:
    - Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease bottoms of muffin cups. Mix dry and add wet ingredients. Sprinkle ¼ tsp sugar on each muffin. Bake 15-20 minutes. Makes 24.
---
